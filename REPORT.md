# Online Retail Sales Analysis Report

## Project Overview

This project analyzes online retail sales data using Power BI to identify key trends, customer behavior, and revenue performance.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of business performance, including revenue trends, top customers, and country-level analysis.

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPI)

The business generated:

- Total Revenue: £8.8M
- Total Invoices: 18,000
- Total Unique Customers: 4,335
- Average Order Value: £476.75

These metrics indicate strong sales performance and consistent customer activity.

---

## Monthly Revenue Analysis

Revenue increased steadily throughout the year, with significant growth in the final quarter.

Key observations:

- Lowest revenue occurred early in the year
- Strong growth began around month 9
- Peak revenue was reached in month 11 (£1.14M)
- High performance continued in month 12

This suggests seasonal demand, likely influenced by holiday sales.

---

## Revenue by Country

The United Kingdom generated the highest revenue by a large margin.

Top countries:

1. United Kingdom — £7.27M
2. Netherlands — £283K
3. EIRE — £262K
4. Germany — £205K
5. France — £184K

The UK is clearly the primary market, representing the majority of total revenue.

---

## Customer Analysis

The top customer generated £279K in revenue.

Key insight:

- A small number of customers contribute significantly to total revenue
- This suggests the presence of high-value customers

This information can be used to identify VIP customers and improve retention strategies.

---

## Product Performance

Top products generated substantial revenue.

Best-performing product:

- PAPER CRAFT, LITTLE BIRDIE — £168K

This indicates strong demand for specific product categories.

---

## Key Insights

- Revenue shows strong upward trend throughout the year
- Sales peak in Q4, suggesting seasonal patterns
- UK is the dominant market
- A small number of customers generate high revenue
- Certain products significantly outperform others

---

## Conclusion

This dashboard provides valuable insights into sales performance, customer behavior, and revenue trends.

The analysis can support business decisions such as:

- targeting high-value customers
- focusing on high-performing products
- optimizing seasonal sales strategies
